Meet Daniel

Daniel grew up in Colorado Springs, Colorado. He went to college at the University of Wyoming, where he earned a degree in Wildlife Biology and Fisheries Management while also being in the AROTC program.

He began guiding in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem in 2015 while also serving in the Wyoming and Idaho National Guards as a Field Artillery Officer.

Daniel also has experience guiding tours in Alaska and Africa. His passion for sharing his knowledge of wildlife and photography has made guiding in the Greater Yellowstone Ecosystem a dream job.
